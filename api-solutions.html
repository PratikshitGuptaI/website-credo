CloudNow’s API Solution for Business Expansion

  • A prominent healthcare services company was having trouble with expanding their business using their existing legacy platform. Onboarding new customers and functionalities was becoming a huge challenge. They needed an automated solution that would help them maintain patient records and keep track of patient progress, filing of medical reports and other patient information. The healthcare services company also required a simple and user-friendly UI that would be easy to operate.
  • CloudNow considered the drawbacks of their monolithic architecture and their need to improve customer experience.
  • We deployed a microservices – API framework approach that helped resolve these challenges:
    • Gradually moved the legacy system in increments to an agile method that accommodates new applications
    • Deployed API framework for IaaS to increase failure resilience and flexibility
    • Enabled data distribution to other platforms and automated data services operations
  • This API and microservices based deployment helped the healthcare service easily add new customers and functionalities setting them up for business expansion.

CloudNow's API Model

CloudNow follows an approach of assess-strategize-deliver while developing the API model that would best suit your business needs.

  • Our expert team goes to work on assessing your existing system, the applications that you require to accelerate your services, partner APIs, and your internal processes in order to understand your integration needs
  • We develop intuitive interface designs with consistency across multiple platforms that enable a seamless and excellent customer experience
  • Our API development is process-oriented and focuses on delivery to maximize efficiency and minimize time to market
  • We set up automated testing frameworks to ensure high quality in terms of performance, usability and security
  • We make API management and monitoring easy for you and your application developers with our Data & Analytics tool that provides a dashboard to monitor usage and performance
